ABOUT US

A LITTLE ABOUT US

New Baldwin International Degree college came into existence in the Year 2007 by visionary Dr. T. Venugopal to impart quality education for students. Affiliated to Bangalore North University, it accords undergraduate Program in BCOM, BBA & BCA to equip students with the necessary skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

“Service and service to mankind is the highest form of worship” This is true in case of Dr. T Venugopal. Monetary wealth is not a criterion to be a part of this institution. Students from all walks of life are being given education with minimum fees.

NBIDC is adduced with digital and infrastructural facilities that ensures holistic development of the students.

Sophisticated and well-advanced computer lab, library, spacious class rooms, seminar & conference hall are some of the key highlights of this institute. NBIDC is renowned for world class academics and extra-curricular activities. Intercollegiate competition, social awareness campaign, Humanitarian deeds and Research Initiatives are few key features of our college.

MESSAGE TO STUDENTS



“INTELLIGENCE with CHARACTER is the goal of true EDUCATION”

Education empowers a WOMAN and enlarges the horizon of her mind while it energizes a society and enables a MAN to earn his living with respect and pride. I, greatly rejoice and proudly place on record the fact that “NEW BALDWIN INTERNATIONAL DEGREE COLLEGE” , a part of NEW BALDWIN GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS, the visionary dream of its founder chairman Dr.T.VENUGOPAL, a humble educationist whose little mission to transform a small school into a big institution of glorious past and great future.

We the NEW BALDWINITES, are endowed with progressive futuristic outlook and aims at continual growth in the quality of all academic activities with a sense of commitment to fully meet the expectations of the students, parents and society at large.

Our college nurtures individual development of each and every student. We follow “MENTOR SYSTEM” under which each student is put into the multipronged web of a professor. We accord prime importance to the behavioural discipline, moral integrity and cognitive developments of our students.

The departments of NCC, NSS, Career guidance cell, Placement & Training , centre for women empowerment offer integrated services for the multifaceted progress of our students. Our faculties not only strive to teach academic programmes but also offers life skills needed for students self development which are highly resourceful.

I wish the very best of fortune, health, peace and prosperity to all those who contribute to the noble task of spreading education and its manifest qualities, aims and objectives.
